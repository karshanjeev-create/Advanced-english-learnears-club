Our Story

About AELC

From the English Enhancement League to the Advanced English Learners' Club — a legacy of confident communicators.

History

Founded over 12 years ago as the English Enhancement League, the club has run workshops, seminars, debates and outreach programs every academic year. In 2026 we adopted a new identity — the Advanced English Learners' Club (AELC) — to reflect how far our members have grown, while preserving every report, photo and memory from our journey.

Vision

To be Ramco Institute of Technology's definitive platform for communication excellence — where every student, regardless of background, can find their voice in English.

Mission

To build fluency, confidence and leadership through structured practice — workshops, mock interviews, competitions and daily communication drills.
